About Me

Hello! My name is Marcus Chung and I'm a second year Engineering Science student at the University of Toronto. I have a keen interest in both psychiatry and data science, and am looking for ways to combine the two. During high school, I volunteered a lot in hospitals and elderly care centers and got to hear from a lot of patients about how their illness affected their lives. I ended up continuing to read book and listen to podcasts about mental illnesses and psychiatry grew into an area I became very invested in, especially as diseases like Alzheimer's are very common in my family. A particular topic I'm interested in is using data to dispel or correct past misinformation and stereotypes about mental conditions. I'm currently looking for research oppurtunities

As for other projects, I'm currently trying to make my own game. Growing up, I loved stories. I spent much of my time playing video games, reading books, and volunteering at places where I could talk to new people, and hear about their lives. Making my own stories has been a dream of mine for a long time, and the first chapters start now.
